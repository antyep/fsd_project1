At VLC26 Restaurant, in Aldaia (Valencia), we prepare Mediterranean gastronomy, paying attention to its roots and using seasonal products. We try to ensure that our menu is nourished with the products available on the daily market. We love rice dishes, which are one of the strong points of VLC26.

We also have local meats and fresh fish of the day, a combination that we hope will satisfy the desires of all those who visit our restaurant. We also have a daily set menu at a fair price that does not skimp on quality. We also have group menus to suit all needs. If you have an event do not hesitate to contact us.
